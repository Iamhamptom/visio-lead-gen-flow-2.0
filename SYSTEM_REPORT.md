## WHAT CLIENTS GET

### Signal Intelligence (23 buying signal types)
We detect when someone in your area is about to buy — before they walk into a dealership:
- Job changes & promotions (new salary = new car)
- Lease expirations (must buy within 30 days)
- Relocations & expat arrivals (need transport)
- New businesses (need company vehicles)
- Funding rounds (cash for fleet)
- Insurance write-offs (need replacement NOW)
- Weddings, babies, graduations (life changes)
- Social media intent (posts about wanting a car)
- Housing developments (new residents)
- Birthday milestones (self-reward purchases)
